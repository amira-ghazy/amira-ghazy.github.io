01

Approach

Most people who can train a model can't tell you whether its scores actually mean anything. Most people who understand measurement can't build the model.

My training sits on both sides — a master's in industrial-organizational psychology with an advanced quantitative methods focus, plus hands-on engineering with ML and large language models.

I'm most useful where human judgment meets automated scoring: model evaluation, people analytics, and assessment systems that have to be defensible, not just accurate.
